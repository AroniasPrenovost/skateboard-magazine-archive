About

I'm trying to commit to uploading 1 magazine a week, but still figuring out the best process to get clear scans without damaging the magazine. Right now I'm loosening the pages from the outer binding to ensure a flat scan surface. I hate to mess up the physical magazine, but I suppose I'll clip the pages together when I'm done and put them back in their boxes.

I apologize in advance as there were a few years in middle school where I cut out (the best) pictures from the magazines and put them in my own binders. I will try to call out these magazines when I get to them. If you send me your magazine I will gladly re-upload and submit, or I'll upload the custom binders if I can find them.

Interested in contributing?

Send questions, suggestions, or scanned files to aronprenovostmktg@gmail.com.
